About Xperio

Connecting travelers with authentic local experiences since 2018

At Xperio, we believe travel should be transformative, not just transactional. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between travelers and local communities, fostering cultural exchange and sustainable tourism practices.

Founded by a team of passionate travelers and local experts, we've built a platform that empowers communities to share their heritage while providing visitors with authentic, immersive experiences that go beyond typical tourism.
